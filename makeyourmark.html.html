About Make Your Mark B&D CIC

Our Mission

Make Your Mark B&D CIC empowers vulnerable individuals and young people across Barking & Dagenham by tackling food insecurity and delivering inclusive educational programmes, wellbeing support, and safe community spaces that foster growth and opportunity.

Our Services

Supporting Our Community, One Service at a Time

Food Club

We operate a referral-based food club offering affordable, nutritious food for local families experiencing hardship. Members receive weekly food packages alongside support for financial advice, housing help, and access to wellbeing services.

  • Access to discounted food weekly
  • Support with budgeting and financial advice
  • Guidance toward other local support services

Youth Education & Mentoring

Through creative workshops, leadership training, and peer support programmes, we help young people in Barking & Dagenham gain confidence, explore their talents, and build brighter futures.

  • Creative arts and performance workshops
  • Leadership and personal development
  • School holiday youth activities

Employment & Skills Support

We connect local residents with CV workshops, digital skills training, and employment opportunities—helping individuals take the next step in their professional journey.

  • Workplace training
  • One-on-one CV and job application support
  • Job search resources and referrals
